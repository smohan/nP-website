Privacy Policy

Last modified: May 30, 2012


Information We Collect
When you share information with us, for example by creating an account on nP.Connect, or requesting your name added to our mailing list, your data is only used in the manner in which is obvious to each specific type:for record keeping, reference for admission or contact, or establishing your digital identity in our administration system. We never collect personally identifiable information about you from any of our for-sale applications. We do track web analytics that can help us understand where our web traffic is coming from, and what technology is used to access our site. These analytics do not contain any personally identifiable information about you.
Information we share
We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of Nonpareil Institute unless you are participating in a special research project, or other auxiliary event outside of the normal operations at the Institute. You will have signed authorizing forms stating use of the data specific to the event.
